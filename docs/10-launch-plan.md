# Launch Plan

## Launch strategy

Do not launch nationwide. Start in one small geography or one controlled community where feedback is real.

Good first launch zones:
- one Hyderabad locality
- Warangal/NITW area
- Suryapet
- one apartment/community group
- one college campus

## Pre-launch checklist

- Admin dashboard works.
- Issue approval works.
- Evidence upload works.
- AI structuring works.
- Complaint draft works.
- Public issue page works.
- Support/verify works.
- Abuse reporting path exists.
- Privacy disclaimer exists.
- Emergency disclaimer exists.

## 30-day beta target

- 50 users
- 20 created issues
- 10 approved issues
- 5 complaint drafts
- 2 escalated issues
- 1 real resolution/progress story

## Acquisition channels

### Local WhatsApp groups

Message:

> We built a simple platform to report local civic issues with photos, gather support, and generate proper complaint drafts. Testing it for our area. Try raising one real issue.

### Local Instagram/X pages

Share before/after case stories, not generic product ads.

### Student groups

Start with campus/locality issues:
- streetlights
- roads
- bus stops
- public safety
- drainage
- garbage

### Apartment groups

Positioning:

> Replace messy WhatsApp complaints with trackable issue cards.

## Launch content ideas

1. Before/after issue story.
2. Why posting on X is not enough.
3. How to raise a proper civic complaint.
4. What makes an issue verifiable.
5. Local issue of the week.

## Beta feedback questions

Ask:
- Was this easier than posting on WhatsApp/X?
- Did the AI summary help?
- Did you understand the next action?
- Would you share the case page?
- What stopped you from submitting?

## Kill criteria

Consider pivoting if after 30–45 days:
- users do not create real issues
- evidence upload rate is low
- nobody shares/supports case pages
- complaint drafts are not used

## Strong positive signal

The strongest signal is:

> People share a case page outside the app and ask others to support it.
